Present Tense: -ER Verbs

Regular -er verbs follow a predictable pattern in the present tense — once you know it, you can conjugate hundreds of verbs like comer, beber, and aprender without memorizing each one individually.

comer — to eat
PronounConjugation
yocomo
túcomes
él / ella / ustedcome
nosotroscomemos
vosotroscoméis
ellos / ellas / ustedescomen
“
Nosotros comemos paella los domingos.
We eat paella on Sundays.

Notice the pattern: -o, -es, -e, -emos, -éis, -en. This same ending set applies to every regular -er verb in Spanish.
